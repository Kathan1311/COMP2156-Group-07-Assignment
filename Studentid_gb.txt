George Brown College is a public college of applied arts and technology located in downtown Toronto, Ontario, Canada. It was chartered in 1966 by the government of Ontario and opened the next year. The college is named after George Brown, a prominent Canadian politician and one of the Fathers of Confederation.
Academic Programs.
George Brown offers a wide range of programs across various fields, including:
• Arts, Design & Information Technology:
Programs like Honours Bachelor of Brand Design and Honours Bachelor of Digital
Experience Design.
• Business: Programs such as Honours
Bachelor of Commerce (Financial Services) and Honours Bachelor of Business Administration (Business
Analytics).
